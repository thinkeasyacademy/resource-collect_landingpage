Do I need a Google account?

Yes. You need a Google account to upload files via Google Form. It helps keep the upload secure and reduces spam.

Can I upload multiple files?

Yes. Upload your lecture sheets for the same course together (PDF/PPT/DOC etc.).

What file types are accepted?

PDF, DOC/DOCX, PPT/PPTX formats.

How will my files be used?

We use lecture sheets to prepare structured, helpful course videos for students.
